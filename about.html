About Us

We're on a mission to simplify healthcare and make it accessible for everyone.

Our Mission

Care4You exists to make high-quality healthcare clear and affordable. We focus on preventive services, transparent pricing, and member-first support.

Our Story

Founded by clinicians and technologists, Care4You started with a simple idea: simplify access to primary care and make costs predictable for families and small businesses.

Our Values

Transparency

Compassion

Patient-centered care at every touchpoint.

Community

Partnering with local clinics to serve neighborhoods.
