Frequently asked questions

How do I enroll?

To enroll, select a plan on the Plans page and contact our team. We offer guided enrollment and employer plans.

What does telehealth cover?

Telehealth covers most primary care visits for minor illnesses, routine follow-ups, and mental health check-ins depending on your plan.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes — plan changes are possible during open enrollment or qualifying life events. Contact support for help.
